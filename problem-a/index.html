Kujo Jotaro

The character kujo Jotaro belongs to a popular Japanese anime tv show called Jojo's Bizarre Adventure. Currently, the show has 6 total seasons. The original manga of this show is already finished which contains 8 parts with 6 parts already covered by the show. So, 2 more seasons are expected to release in the near future until the finale. kujo Jotaro's first appearance is in season 3 which he plays the role of the protagonist. He plays a supporting role or is briefly mentioned in all of the later seasons until season 6.
